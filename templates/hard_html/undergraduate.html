General Info

🎓

Motivation

The admission screening procedure at UAP aims to find the most deserving students for each department. It is also important for students to find out their own skill sets which will suggest and guide them in choosing appropriate career paths.

🎓

General Timing and Procedure

  • Spring Semester (in general): July to December
  • Fall Semester (in general): January to June
🎓

Admission Test

Applicants must sit for an admission test conducted by the university, unless exempted by eligibility criteria or credit transfer from equivalent institutions.

🎓

Transfer Students

Transfer students may be considered based on their previous academic records and availability of space in the desired department.

🎓

International Students

International applicants must follow the same admission procedures, with appropriate credential equivalency and visa requirements fulfilled.

Admission Info

🎓

Admission Criteria

B.Sc. Eng. in CSE

Minimum GPA Requirements

  • Total GPA in SSC & HSC = 7.50
  • Total GPA in O & A level = 7.50
  • (Minimum GPA of 2.5 in 5 subjects in O level and 2 subjects in A level)

Subject Requirements: Must have Physics, Mathematics in HSC (or equivalent) and Chemistry in SSC or HSC (or equivalent).

📝

Special Notes

  • Admit Card: Collect from UAP admission office.
  • Admission Notice / Test Schedules: Available at the office or online.
  • Diploma Students: Must have minimum GPA of 3.0 and apply for respective department.
  • H.S.C. Appeared Candidates: Must meet minimum requirements after HSC result.
  • Students with Study Gap: Max 2 years (or 1 for Pharmacy).

Deviation between documents and info will cancel admission.
