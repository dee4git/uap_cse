Admission Info

Admission Criteria

B.Sc. Eng. in CSE

Minimum GPA Requirements

  • Total GPA in SSC & HSC = 7.50
  • Total GPA in O & A level = 7.50
  • Minimum GPA of 2.5 in 5 subjects in O level and 2 in A level

Subject Requirements: Must have Physics, Mathematics in HSC and Chemistry in SSC or HSC.

Special Notes

  • Admit Card: Collect from UAP admission office.
  • Admission Notice / Test Schedules: Available at the office or online.
  • Diploma Students: Must have a minimum GPA of 3.0.
  • H.S.C. Appeared Candidates: Must meet requirements after HSC results.
  • Students with Study Gap: Max 2 years (or 1 for Pharmacy).

Deviation between documents and info will cancel admission.
